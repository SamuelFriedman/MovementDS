# Notation

## Preliminary movement notation (inspired by EWMN, but with ASCII in mind)

## Directions
* < Leftwards
* > Rightwards
* ^ Upwards
* v Downwards

## Rotations
* (x y)
* [x y]

## Angles
* + (Third)
* ++ (Half)
* # (Two thirds)

## Directions
* -> is one path
* ^6 >3 is one movement amount

## Rotations:
* {v} Clockwise (CW)
* |v| Counterclockwise (CCW)
